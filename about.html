About Us

Climate change renewal (CRenew)! is a social enterprise that has successfully developed coalitions among partners like schools, universities and other organizations to advocate, train and link up various agencies in the fight against climate change through waste management, renewable energy promotion, promoting the sustainable development goals 17SDGs and pollution control towards ensuring environmental sustainability and to make sure our current actions does not affects future intentions

While serving the world in combating climate change through pollution control with our goods and services (renewable energy research, waste management creating environmentally friendly policies and 17SDGs advocacy) we are absolutely aware that the effects of climate change are on a daily increase, causing 5 and efforts must be made in other to reduce it to its barest minimum. CRenew tagged “Eco Clean” which started in 2017 came to be through the development of an idea on “Particulate Matters (PM)” These are emissions (pollutants) from fossils (burning) and the inability to manage waste.
